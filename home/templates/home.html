About Me

I am a Southern California native with a degree in Information Systems Security from Westwood Technical College. My passion for technology began in childhood, driven by a curiosity for electronics.


I currently serve as a Senior IT Infrastructure Engineer at The Walsh Group Corporation, where I design and maintain secure and scalable IT infrastructure. Previously, I was an Information Technology Project Manager, overseeing a $2.5 billion construction project and managing IT support for over 400 users. Before joining The Walsh Group, I spent nine years at Kiewit Corporation in various technology roles, contributing to numerous infrastructure projects across the Western United States.
